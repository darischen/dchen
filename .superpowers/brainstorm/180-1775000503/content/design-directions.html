Dark Refined

Vibe: Hacker aesthetic meets sophistication. Evolve your current dark theme with intentional typography, better spacing, refined accents.

Best for: Technical credibility. "I write code, I understand systems."

Projects shine: Code samples, ML experiments, low-level work stands out against dark background.

$ portfolio --mode clarity
Clean, spacious layout
Typography-forward design
High readability, minimal color

Light Minimal

Vibe: Confident. You don't need flashy design because the work speaks. Apple/Stripe energy.

Best for: Design maturity. "I have taste and restraint."

Projects shine: UI work, dashboards, anything visual. Images pop on light background.

$ portfolio --mode balanced
Warm, inviting aesthetic
Good contrast, easy on eyes
Technical + approachable

Warm Technical

Vibe: Sophisticated but human. You're technical AND approachable. Less sterile than pure light/dark.

Best for: Versatility signal. "I work across domains and communicate well."

Projects shine: Everything. Warm tones work great with diverse content types and color diagrams.

Which direction resonates?

Dark Refined: If you want to lean into the technical/systems aspect

Light Minimal: If you want to emphasize design maturity and confidence

Warm Technical: If you want to balance technical credibility with approachability across your diverse work
